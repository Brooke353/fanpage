The Amazing Game of Volleyball

The Best Sport

About

Volleyball is fun game consisting of a net and ball. The objest of the game is to get to 25 points first. You must win 3 out of the 5 sets to win the whole game. There are 2 teams and 6 players on the court at a time. The ball must not land on your court and you cannot hit it out of bounds. Simple ways to keep the ball in the air are passing, setting, and hitting. You want the ball to land on the opposing teams court and not on yours.

Positions

Setting

Setting is played by using your hands and pushing the ball high and forward. The setter commonly uses this skill but anyone on the court can as well. Setting is needed to set up the hitters or getting a ball above your head. Without setting it would be hard to score points.

↑

Passing

Passing is defensive skill and the most simple. You pass by having your forearms come in contact with the ball and getting the ball high and far. You most commonly use this in the back row but it can also be found in the front row. Without passing the ball would hit the ground most of the time.

↑

Hitting

Hitting is where you hit the ball towards the ground on the opposing teams court. You essentially hit or spike the ball downwards so it is harder for the other team to get the ball. This skill is commonly used in the front row but can also be found in the back row. Hitting helps you score points or make it harder for the other team to keep the ball in the air.
